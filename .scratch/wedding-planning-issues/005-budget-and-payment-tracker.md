# Budget and Payment Tracker

## What to build

Add Budget tracking for the whole Wedding and individual Events, with Budget Items for planned or allocated costs and Payments for actual due or paid amounts. The Dashboard should show planned budget, expected spend, paid amount, remaining amount, overdue Payments, and over-budget signals.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] A Planner or Owner can set a whole-Wedding Budget.
- [ ] A Planner or Owner can set Event-level Budgets.
- [ ] A Planner or Owner can create Budget Items linked to the Wedding, Event, Vendor, Planning Area, Ritual, or Item where relevant.
- [ ] Payments can be tracked with amount, due date, paid date, and Payment Status.
- [ ] Payment Status supports Due, Scheduled, Paid, Overdue, and Cancelled.
- [ ] The Dashboard shows budget/payment summary and due or overdue Payments.
- [ ] Tests cover budget rollups, payment status behavior, overdue payment signals, and over-budget detection.
