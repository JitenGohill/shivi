# Invited Collaborator Access and Roles

## What to build

Add invited-only Collaborator access for the single Wedding. The bride starts as an Owner, can invite Collaborators by email, assign Owner, Planner, or Contributor roles, and remove or change Collaborators later. Guests remain planning records only and must not be able to sign in.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] An Owner can invite a Collaborator by email with a selected role.
- [ ] Invited Collaborators can access the Wedding after accepting or using the configured invite flow.
- [ ] Owner, Planner, and Contributor permissions are enforced for representative create, edit, and status-update actions.
- [ ] Guests cannot sign in and are not treated as platform users.
- [ ] Tests prove role-based access for Owner, Planner, and Contributor behavior.
