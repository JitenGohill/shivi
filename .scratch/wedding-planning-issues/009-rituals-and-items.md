# Rituals and Items

## What to build

Add lightweight first-class Rituals and Items. A Planner should be able to create ceremony-specific Rituals within Events, record timing, materials, participants, Notes, and related Tasks, and track physical Items needed for buying, packing, delivery, ownership, or setup.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] A Planner or Owner can add Rituals to an Event.
- [ ] Rituals can track timing/order, participants, materials, Notes, Documents, and related Tasks.
- [ ] A Planner or Owner can create Items needed for the Wedding, Event, Ritual, or Planning Area.
- [ ] Items can track owner, status, location or destination, and setup/packing context.
- [ ] Items can link to Tasks, Budget Items, Payments, and Documents where relevant.
- [ ] Tests cover Ritual creation, Item tracking, and relationships to Tasks/Documents/Budget records.

## Blocked by

- Event and Task Planning Loop
- Documents and Notes Across Planning Records
