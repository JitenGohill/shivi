# Meal Preferences and Lightweight Seating Plans

## What to build

Add Meal Preferences and lightweight Seating Plans to guest planning. The slice should support guest-level food planning details, Event-level catering count rollups, and simple Event-specific table, row, or section assignments without a floorplan editor.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] A Planner or Owner can record Meal Preferences for a Guest.
- [ ] Meal Preferences can capture vegetarian, Jain, vegan, allergies, kids meal, or freeform catering notes.
- [ ] Event views show catering counts based on event-level RSVPs and Meal Preferences.
- [ ] A Planner or Owner can create a lightweight Seating Plan for an Event.
- [ ] Guests or Guest Groups can be assigned to tables, rows, or sections.
- [ ] No drag-and-drop floorplan editor is introduced.
- [ ] Tests cover catering rollups and Seating Plan assignments.

## Blocked by

- Guest Groups, Invitations, and Event-Level RSVPs
