# Planning Areas

## What to build

Add Planning Areas as flexible sections for topics that are not naturally Events or Vendors, such as outfits, decor, food, gifts, rituals, logistics, music, beauty, and guest experience. Planning Areas should support Leads, Tasks, Notes, Documents, Budget Items, and Vendors where relevant.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] A Planner or Owner can create and edit Planning Areas.
- [ ] A Planning Area can have a Lead.
- [ ] Tasks can be linked to a Planning Area.
- [ ] Notes and Documents can be attached to a Planning Area.
- [ ] Budget Items and Vendors can be associated with a Planning Area where relevant.
- [ ] Planning Areas appear in navigation and can be filtered or summarized.
- [ ] Tests cover Planning Area creation, Lead assignment, related records, and permissions.

## Blocked by

- Event and Task Planning Loop
- Documents and Notes Across Planning Records
