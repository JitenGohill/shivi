# Accommodation and Transport Planning

## What to build

Add planning-only Accommodation and Transport records. The app should help Collaborators coordinate hotel blocks, room needs, assignments, dates, shuttles, pickups, car assignments, arrival windows, route Notes, and related Tasks without directly booking lodging, flights, or rides.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] A Planner or Owner can create Accommodation planning records for Guests or Collaborators.
- [ ] Accommodation can track hotel blocks, room needs, assignments, dates, Notes, Documents, and Tasks.
- [ ] A Planner or Owner can create Transport planning records for Guests, Collaborators, Vendors, or wedding parties.
- [ ] Transport can track shuttles, pickups, car assignments, arrival windows, route Notes, Documents, Vendors, and Tasks.
- [ ] Accommodation and Transport items can appear in Timeline where dates/times exist.
- [ ] No direct hotel, flight, or ride booking is introduced.
- [ ] Tests cover Accommodation and Transport creation, relationships, Timeline inclusion, and permissions.

## Blocked by

- Guest Groups, Invitations, and Event-Level RSVPs
- Timeline, Reminders, and Attention Needed
