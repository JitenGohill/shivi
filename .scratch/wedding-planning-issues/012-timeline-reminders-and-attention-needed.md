# Timeline, Reminders, and Attention Needed

## What to build

Add a chronological Timeline, in-app Reminders, and richer Attention Needed signals. The Timeline should show Events, Rituals, Tasks, Payments, and important schedule items across the Wedding. Reminders should be in-app only. Attention Needed should highlight concrete planning problems that require Collaborator action.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] A Collaborator can view a Timeline of Events, Rituals, Tasks, Payments, and schedule items.
- [ ] A Planner or Owner can set reminder dates for Tasks, Payments, Events, or Planning Areas.
- [ ] The app surfaces in-app Reminders for upcoming or overdue work.
- [ ] Attention Needed highlights overdue Tasks, blocked Tasks, overdue Payments, missing venue, missing contract, budget overrun, RSVP follow-up, and Rituals missing required Items where the underlying records exist.
- [ ] Dashboard Attention Needed cards link back to source records.
- [ ] No external email, SMS, WhatsApp, or push notifications are introduced.
- [ ] Tests cover Timeline ordering, Reminder visibility, and Attention Needed generation.

## Blocked by

- Event and Task Planning Loop
- Budget and Payment Tracker
- Guest Groups, Invitations, and Event-Level RSVPs
- Rituals and Items
