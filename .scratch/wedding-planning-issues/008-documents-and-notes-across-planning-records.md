# Documents and Notes Across Planning Records

## What to build

Add Documents and Notes as shared planning context that can attach to major planning records. Documents may be uploaded files or external links. Notes are freeform planning information near the relevant Wedding, Event, Vendor, Budget Item, Payment, Guest, Guest Group, Task, Ritual, Item, Planning Area, Accommodation, or Transport record where available.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] A Planner or Owner can add a Document as a file or external link.
- [ ] A Planner or Owner can attach Documents to supported planning records.
- [ ] A Planner, Owner, or permitted Contributor can add Notes to supported planning records.
- [ ] Attached Documents and Notes are visible from the record they belong to.
- [ ] Vendor records can show quotes, contracts, menus, receipts, or reference files through Documents.
- [ ] Tests cover creating, attaching, viewing, and permission behavior for Documents and Notes.
